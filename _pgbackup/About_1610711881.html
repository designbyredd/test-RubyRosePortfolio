About

intro

I have a First Class degree in Architecture from the University of Sheffield. I enjoy presenting work and collaborating with others. Alongside my creative abilities and interest in design, I pay attention to context and sustainability. I look beyond the aesthetics of a building, and concentrate on the design’s responsibility as a whole.
